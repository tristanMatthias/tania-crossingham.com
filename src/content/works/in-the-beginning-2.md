This is another version of this esoteric and powerful verse, interpreted by many people in many ways. Allow it to settle within and speak to your heart.

The image uses elements from several bibles created during the Romanesque period in the twelfth century. The text is from John 1:1-5 in the Bible.

Hand painted on pergamenata paper using gouache, ink and embellished with 23ct gold leaf.

Original Image size 21 x 29.5 cm
