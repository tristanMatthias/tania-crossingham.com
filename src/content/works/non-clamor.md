“Not clamour, but love, Not talking, but pledge, Not flagellation, but heart, Make music in ear of God” (Cut into the choir stall of San Damiano in Assisi by Thomas of Celano – a devoted disciple of St Francis )

Based on Illuminations done in Bruges and Ghent in the late fifteenth century. Hand painted on calfskin parchment using watercolours, ink and gold gouache

Original Image size 14.5 x 19 cm
