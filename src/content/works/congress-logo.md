Logo Design

Designed for the 35th International Congress of Genealogical and Heraldic Sciences held at Clare College, Cambridge University August 2022. This design features the arms of Clare College, Cambridge University, and the Cambridge University Genealogical and Heraldic Society.

Hand painted on hot press watercolour paper using gouache

Original Image size 21 x 21 cm
