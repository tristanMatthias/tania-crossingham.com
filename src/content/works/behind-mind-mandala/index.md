Beneath the negative, cluttered thoughts that cycle frantically in the prison of mind lies the stillness, peace and clarity that is your Divine essence. You are not your thoughts, your emotions or your body but Divine awareness experiencing life. Recede behind the smallness of mind into the vastness and power of your Being that expands to fill the universe.
