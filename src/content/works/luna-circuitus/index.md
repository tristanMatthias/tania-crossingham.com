The moon and it's cycles can assist you in your manifestations. In the dark of the new moon, plant the seeds of your intent and then in the Light of the full moon work with this intent and create actions to bring your intent into being.

Hand painted with gouache on paper marbled with acrylic. Original image size 13 × 17 cm.
