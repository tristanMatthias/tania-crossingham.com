The heart is a sacred and magical place that we often neglect to go to. We get so caught up with our daily plans, our thoughts that just never stop demanding attention, and our emotions, that we forget about this secret place within. If you can take the time to enter this space of the soul and just stop... then life begins to take on a whole new perspective.

Artwork is inspired by Romanesque book from the twelfth century.

Words by Tania Crossingham

Hand painted on calfskin parchment using watercolours, ink and embellished with gold leaf.

Original Image size 18 x 23.5cm
