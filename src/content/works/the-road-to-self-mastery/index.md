The only difference between a master and a novice is that the master has made so many more mistakes and learned from them. Mistakes are merely golden opportunities to grow and should not be judged as failure. They are a necessary part of life’s journey; each one a jewel in the crown of self mastery.

Image inspired by the Arnstein Bible, from the Arnstein Abbey in Germany produced in 1172. Romanesque style illumination.

Words by Tania Crossingham.

Hand painted on calfskin parchment using watercolours, ink.
