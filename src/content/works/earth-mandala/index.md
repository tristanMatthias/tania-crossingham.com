Earth is a jewel of the universe. Her myriad of life in all its dimensions, a testament to Divine Creativity.

The elemental beings of earth, air, fire and water hold the energy for all life on earth while Gaia, the planetary consciousness holds the energy for the planet itself. Humanity is intricately linked with all of these life forces and it is our job as caretakers of the earth to manage it's resources wisely and respectfully.
