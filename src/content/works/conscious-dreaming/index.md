The art of conscious dreaming is an ancient and powerful tool to bring into manifestation your soul's deepest longing and the whispers from your divine essence. Have the courage to dream big, allowing yourself to become and create, more than you believed possible. As you sleep, weave the universal symbols into your dream creation, and watch the magic blossom.

Hand painted on paper with watercolour, ink and embellished with gold leaf. Original image size 42 × 30 cm.
