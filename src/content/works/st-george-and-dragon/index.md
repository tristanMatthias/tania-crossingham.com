Chivalry and the respect for all life has been lost in our current world which has led to a growing darkness. In order to enlighten our world, the ancient principles of chivalry needs to be restored, reclaimed and embeded into the consciousness of mankind. Our world needs heroes again. The symbol of St George serves to remind us all that we can be our own hero.

Hand painted on parchment with gouache and 23ct gold leaf.
