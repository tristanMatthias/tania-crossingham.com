The Latin quote translates as " Divine inspiration knows no limitation".

Anything and everything is possible. It is only the human mind that creates the limitations. Learn to move beyond the mind into the realm of limitless possibilities.

Inspired by designs from twelfth century manuscripts.

Words by Tania Crossingham

Hand painted on calfskin parchment using watercolour, gouache, ink and embellished with gold leaf.

Original Image size 15x 15cm
