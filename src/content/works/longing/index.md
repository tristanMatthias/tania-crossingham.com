Standing under the night sky, feeling the energy of the universe flowing on the wind, I feel the pull of the stars and a deep longing grows within. An ache of homesickness is realized and a longing to understand my connection to all of this more clearly gnaws away at me.

Hand drawn on watercolour paper with graphite. Original image size 21 × 30 cm.
