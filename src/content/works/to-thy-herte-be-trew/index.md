Written and painted for Simon, the love of my life.

The unconditional love between two people is an example of Divine love in action. Being true to your heart and listening to the voice of the soul will go a long way towards building a strong foundation for two people’s future together. Relationships are wonderful opportunities to learn and grow together, teaching each other through love, patience and consideration. They allow us to discover more about ourselves as we learn to balance our personal boundaries and self respect with the other person’s needs and ways of being.

Inspired by manuscripts from the fifteenth century.

Hand painted on deerskin parchment using gouache, ink and embellished with gold leaf.

Original Image size 20.5 x 28 cm
