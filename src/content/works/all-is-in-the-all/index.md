“All is in the All; the All is in all “This quote is from ‘The Kybalion’ written by three Initiates. It means, ALL creation rests in the consciousness of The ALL, God or the Source. THE ALL is an integral part of ALL creation. It is an expression of the primary universal law... “The Law of One” It is similar to the famous quote “That which is above is as that which is below” from the Emerald Tablet. The image behind the the text is an adaption of the yin yang symbol.

Watercolours, ink and gold leaf on paper
