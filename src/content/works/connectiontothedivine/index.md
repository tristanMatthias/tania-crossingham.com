So often we live in a state of atonement believing we somehow have to earn our way back to the Divine. We are not “sinners”, separated from the Divine by our misdeeds. The only separation between us and Divine comes from our own minds, beliefs and lack of understanding. That connection is an intrinsic part of us waiting to be acknowledged.

Hand painted on goatskin parchment using gouache, ink and embellished withgold leaf and shell gold.

Words by Tania Crossingham

Original Image size 19 x 26 cm
