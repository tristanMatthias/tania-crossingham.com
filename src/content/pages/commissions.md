A commission is a collaboration. Whether it is a grant of arms, an illuminated poem, wedding vows or a gift to mark a life, each work is designed from scratch around your story — and painted entirely by hand.

## How it works

### 1 — Conversation

Write with your idea: the text, the occasion, the person. We discuss scope, scale and budget, and I sketch an initial direction.

### 2 — Design

You receive a pencil design for approval before any paint or gold is laid. Nothing proceeds until you are delighted with the composition.

### 3 — The making

Gesso, gold leaf, burnishing, pigment — the piece is built up over weeks or months depending on complexity. You receive progress photographs along the way.

### 4 — Delivery

The finished work is shipped worldwide, ready for framing — an heirloom made to last centuries.

## Begin

Send a note describing what you have in mind via the [contact page](../contact/). There is no obligation — every commission starts with a conversation.
