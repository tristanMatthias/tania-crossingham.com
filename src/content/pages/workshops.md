Illumination is not a lost art — it is a living one, and it can be learned. Tania teaches students of all levels the full traditional technique: preparing vellum, laying gesso, gilding with 23-carat gold leaf, and painting with gouache in the medieval manner.

## Online — School of Illumination

Step-by-step video courses you can follow from anywhere in the world, at your own pace, with feedback on your work.

## In person

Intensive workshops are held in Finland and on occasional international tours. Numbers are kept small so every student gets individual attention.
